O/IGCSE Computer Science

This course prepares students for Cambridge O Level and IGCSE Computer Science exams. It covers all core concepts including programming, logic gates, computer systems, networks, databases, and paper-solving techniques. With real exam strategies, concept-focused teaching, and past paper practice, students will build a strong foundation to achieve high grades.

  • Level: O Level / IGCSE
  • Mode: Online / In-person
  • Instructor: O Level Computer Science Specialist
  • Prerequisites: None
